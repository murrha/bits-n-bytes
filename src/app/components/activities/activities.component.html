Activities

Build Projects

Got a project idea but need guidance on where to start? Want to help out with any of member's current projects? A bit intimidated but want to learn something new?
We have different kinds of projects going on whether it's formed by the organization, a few club members, or even personal projects. This is a safe space where anyone can contribute and learn new skills while creating something awesome!

Hardware Repair

We can do simple repairs or replacements of hardware done by our students through the supervisor of an instructor or licensed-professional. Simple repairs are free, but complex ones will require a fee.
For items we cannot fix or beyond our scope, we will recommend a specialist.

Education

We host classes and worshops related to a wide arrangement of topics within the world of technology. It can be about hardware, software, jobs in technology, common technology terms, etc.
